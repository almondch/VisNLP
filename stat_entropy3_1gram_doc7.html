Word Probability Generator

A probability \(p(t_j)\) of each unigram in a document vector is computed by \( \frac{f(t_j)}{\sum_{j=1}^{D} f(t_j)} \), where \(f(t_j)\) is the frequency of term \(t\) in the document \(j\) and \(D\) is the total number of documents for \(1\leq j \leq D \).
